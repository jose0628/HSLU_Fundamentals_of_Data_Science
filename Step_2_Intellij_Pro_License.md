# Guide to Requesting IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate for Students

JetBrains offers free licenses for students to access their professional development tools, including IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate. Follow these steps to apply for a student license.

---

## 1. Check Your Eligibility
As HSLU student, you are eligible for pro subscription
- You need a valid student email address or proof of enrollment.
- The license is for personal use in educational and non-commercial projects.

## 2. Sign Up for a JetBrains Account
If you don’t already have a JetBrains account:
1. Go to [JetBrains website](https://www.jetbrains.com/)
2. Click on **Sign In** (top right) and then **Create Account**.
3. Fill in your details, including a valid email address.
4. Verify your email after registration.

## 3. Apply for a Free Student License
1. Visit the [JetBrains Student License page](https://www.jetbrains.com/community/education/#students).
2. Click **Apply Now**.
3. Choose one of the verification methods:
   - **Using Your School Email**: If your school email domain is recognized, you will get instant approval.
   - **Using ISIC Card**: If you have an ISIC (International Student Identity Card), you can use it for verification.
   - **Uploading Proof of Enrollment**: If your school email isn’t recognized, upload proof of enrollment (student ID, transcript, or enrollment letter).
4. Fill out the form and submit your request.

## 4. Wait for Approval
- If you use your school email, approval is usually instant.
- If you upload proof manually, approval can take a few days.
- You will receive a confirmation email when your license is approved.

## 5. Download and Activate IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate
1. Go to [JetBrains Download Page](https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/download/).
2. Download **IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate**.
3. Install the software and log in with your JetBrains account.
4. Your student license should be automatically applied.

## 6. Renewing Your License
- The student license is valid for **one year**.
- You can renew it as long as you are still a student.
- Visit the JetBrains Student License page and follow the renewal process before your license expires.

---

## Troubleshooting & Support
- If you have issues with verification, check JetBrains’ [Student License FAQ](https://www.jetbrains.com/community/education/#faq).
- If your school email is not recognized, contact JetBrains support.

By following these steps, you should be able to access IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate for free as a student. Enjoy coding!
