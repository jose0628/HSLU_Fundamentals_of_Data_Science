# AI Platforms: Step-by-Step Account Creation Tutorial

This tutorial will guide you through creating accounts (where available) on several AI platforms: **ChatGPT**, **Gemini**, **Claude**, **Perplexity**, and **DeepSeek**. Please note that we require only the free version.

Please create the account that will be mostly used with your team. (You do not need to get an account for every service)
---

## 1. Creating an Account on ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a product by [OpenAI](https://openai.com). You can use ChatGPT via [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com/).

### Steps:

1. **Go to the ChatGPT website**  
   - In your browser, navigate to [https://chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com).

2. **Click on "Sign up"**  
   - On the landing page, you’ll see an option to “Log in” or “Sign up.”  
   - Click **Sign up**.

3. **Choose your sign-up method**  
   - You can sign up with an email address, Google account, or Microsoft account.
   - If using an email, you will be asked to enter it and create a password.

4. **Verify your email**  
   - You will receive a verification link at the email address you provided.
   - Click the verification link to confirm.

5. **Complete the setup**  
   - You may be asked for additional information (e.g., name, phone number for verification, etc.).
   - Once completed, you should have access to ChatGPT from your account dashboard.

**Tip:** If you have trouble signing up or verifying your account, check your spam or junk folder for the confirmation email, or visit [OpenAI Support](https://help.openai.com/) for assistance.

# Common Troubleshooting Tips

1. **Check Spam/Junk Folders**  
   - Verification emails can land there.
2. **Use a Reliable Email**  
   - Some AI platforms may have issues with temporary or disposable emails.
3. **Try Alternative Browsers**  
   - If you encounter issues, switching from Chrome to Firefox (or vice versa) can sometimes help.
4. **Contact Support**  
   - Most platforms have a help or support page if you’re stuck.

# Conclusion

Creating accounts on AI platforms typically involves:
- Visiting the official website,
- Clicking a **Sign Up** or **Join Waitlist** button,
- Providing an email or using a trusted third-party login (e.g., Google, Microsoft),
- Verifying your email,
- Potentially waiting for approval if the platform is in beta/closed testing.

Each platform’s specific steps may change over time, so always refer to the official website for the most up-to-date instructions. If you encounter any issues, look for a **Help** or **Support** link on the respective platform.
