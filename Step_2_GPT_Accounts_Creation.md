# Step 2: Create an AI Account for Team Work

Applies to: Windows and macOS (browser-based)

## Goal
Create at least one AI account your team will use consistently during the course. You do not need an account on every platform.

## Recommended Choice Strategy
1. Pick one primary platform for your team.
2. Ensure all team members can log in successfully.
3. Use free plans unless your instructor explicitly asks for paid features.

## Universal Sign-Up Flow (works for all listed platforms)
1. Open the official platform URL.
2. Select **Sign up** or **Create account**.
3. Register with email, Google, Microsoft, or Apple (depending on provider options).
4. Verify email or phone if prompted.
5. Sign in and confirm you can start a chat/session.

Windows note: Use Edge or Chrome for best compatibility with identity providers.
macOS note: Safari works, but Chrome is often easiest for multi-account switching.

## Platform-Specific Notes

### ChatGPT
1. Open https://chatgpt.com
2. Create your account and complete any verification prompts.
3. Confirm you can start a new chat.

### Gemini
1. Open https://gemini.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account.
3. Confirm Gemini opens and accepts prompts in your region.

### Claude
1. Open https://claude.ai
2. Create/sign in to your Anthropic account.
3. Complete phone/email verification if requested.

### Perplexity
1. Open https://www.perplexity.ai/
2. Sign up using Google, Apple, or email.
3. Verify you can submit a search/chat query.

### DeepSeek
1. Open https://chat.deepseek.com
2. Create an account or sign in with available methods.
3. Confirm chat access after verification.

## Troubleshooting
- Verification emails missing: check spam/junk first.
- Region restrictions: try an alternative approved platform from this list.
- Phone verification issues: retry with a standard mobile number and no VPN.
- Browser session issues: use private/incognito window and sign in again.

## Team Readiness Checklist
- At least one approved platform selected.
- All team members can sign in.
- Free plan access confirmed.
